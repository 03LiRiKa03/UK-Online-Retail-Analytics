# UK Online Retail Analytics

## Transaction data from an online retail business specializing in unique giftware.

### Goal: 

Analyze data to uncover key products, customer segments, and sales trends.

### Results: 
Cleaned and preprocessed data, segmented customers, identified top products and trends, and provided actionable insights to boost revenue and retention.
